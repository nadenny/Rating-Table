The Method

Every game gets scored on seven dimensions, each from 1 to 5 where 3 is a genuinely average game. Those combine — weighted by what matters to you — into one overall out of 10, where 5 is dead average. The point isn't to be nice; it's to be consistent, so a 7 always means the same thing across every review.
